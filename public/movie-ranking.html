The Best Movies According to Max

My top 3 movies of all time

Avengers: Endgame

A Marvel fan's greatest joy, Endgame is full of beautiful moments and fun callbacks.

Spiderman: Across the Spiderverse

A visual treat, AtS is full of classic Spiderman humor. The movie also introduces some great twists.

Howl's Moving Castle

One of Miyazaki's finest, Howl's features a wonderful soundtrack that enhances a deep and emotional plot.
